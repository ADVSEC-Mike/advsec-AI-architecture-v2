The Invisible Structural Threats

⚖️

Fractured Documentation

Most security networks suffer from scattered, unverified documentation. When device lists do exist, they are often locked in legacy spreadsheets. If that file's password is lost or the data is siloed, you lose technical command over your system. We recover, standardize, and centralize this critical data.

🗜️

The Time Synchronization Drift

A staggering number of enterprise security networks lack synchronized NTP time across their devices. In the physical security world, mismatched timestamps destroy the legal defensibility of your video evidence. This is a critical structural failure, yet easily corrected with proper oversight.

👁️

Storage and Bandwidth are your Greatest Expense

IP cameras ship from the factory with the data throttle wide open. Shockingly, many "high-security" installations operate on these default profiles because a technician considered the job done once a picture appeared on screen. This negligence instantly destroys your storage capacity and chokes network bandwidth.
